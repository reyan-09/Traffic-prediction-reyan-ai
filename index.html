How It Works

1

Data Collection

Our system aggregates data from traffic cameras, sensors, weather reports, historical accident records, and connected vehicles to build a comprehensive picture of road conditions.

2

AI Processing

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze patterns and correlations in the data to identify risk factors and predict potential accident scenarios before they occur.

3

Risk Assessment

The system calculates real-time risk scores for different road segments, taking into account factors like traffic density, weather, time of day, and historical accident rates.

4

Actionable Insights

Authorities receive prioritized alerts and recommendations, while drivers get personalized warnings through navigation apps and connected vehicle systems.
